<h2 id="english">English - Privacy Policy</h2>

<p class="text-xs opacity-75">Last Updated: May 19, 2026</p>

### 1. Introduction

This website (`https://danielart.github.io`) is a personal portfolio and blog owned and operated by Daniel Artola Dominguez. I respect your privacy and am committed to protecting any personal data you may share with me.

### 2. Data Collection & Processing

As a static website hosted on GitHub Pages, we minimize the collection of personal data:

- **Hosting (GitHub Pages):** GitHub may collect server log data (such as your IP address, browser type, and time of visit) to ensure security and maintenance of the hosting service. Please refer to [GitHub's Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement) for more information.
- **Web Analytics:** If you explicitly accept our cookie consent banner, we load Google Tag Manager / Google Analytics to collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics (such as pages visited, session duration, and device types). This helps me understand which content is most engaging.
- **External Links, Substack & Comments:** If you subscribe to my Substack newsletter, follow me on LinkedIn/GitHub, or interact with the comments system (powered by Giscus), your data is processed directly by those respective platforms under their own privacy policies. Giscus uses the GitHub API to authenticate and post comments, meaning your GitHub user data and activity are processed in accordance with GitHub's Privacy Statement.

### 3. Cookies

We only use non-essential analytical cookies if you select **"Accept"** in our Cookie Consent banner. You can decline or clear your browser cookies at any time to withdraw consent.

#### Cookies Used:

| Cookie  | Source           | Purpose                                            | Lifespan |
| :------ | :--------------- | :------------------------------------------------- | :------- |
| `_ga`   | Google Analytics | Distinguishes unique users and session activity.   | 2 years  |
| `_ga_*` | Google Analytics | Maintains session state and user interaction data. | 2 years  |

#### Consent Management:

You can reset your consent preferences at any time by clicking the button below. This will clear your saved choices and reload the page so you can make a new selection:

### 4. Your Rights

Under the GDPR and other privacy regulations, you have the right to access, rectify, or request the deletion of any personal data you have shared. Since we do not store personal data or maintain user accounts, most interactions are completely anonymous.

### 5. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact me at: **danielartoladominguez@gmail.com**

### 2. データの収集と処理

当サイトはGitHub Pagesでホストされている静的ウェブサイトであるため、個人データの収集を最小限に抑えています。

- **ホスティング (GitHub Pages):** GitHubは、サービスのセキュリティとメンテナンスを確保するために、サーバーログデータ（IPアドレス、ブラウザの種類、訪問時間など）を収集する場合があります。詳細は [GitHubプライバシーステートメント](https://docs.github.com/ja/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement) をご参照ください。
- **アクセス解析:** クッキー同意バナーで「同意する」を選択した場合のみ、GoogleタグマネージャーおよびGoogleアナリティクスを読み込み、匿名の統計データ（閲覧ページ、滞在時間、デバイスの種類など）を収集します。これにより、どのコンテンツが読者にとって有益であるかを把握します。
